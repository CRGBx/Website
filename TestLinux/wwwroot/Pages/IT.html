Digital Products & Services

CRGB provides various digital services for customers to support 24x7 Banking.

  • ATM Cards
    In order to facilitate banking transactions during 24*7, CRGB has been issuing the debit cards to its customers. Presently bank is issuing RuPay cards in association with NPCI. Presently we are issuing Platinum cards & Classic debit cards .
  • Mobile Banking
    Our Mobile Banking Application “M-Tej” consists of following features:
    • Inter Bank fund transfer through NEFT / IMPS- P2P, P2A and P2M
    • Transfer of funds within Bank
    • Balance enquiry , Mini Statment
    • Debit Card Hotlisting & Limit Control
    • Green PIN Generation
    The mobile banking application can be downloaded from Google Playstore or Apple Store.
  • Internet Banking

  • Mobile ATM Vans
    Bank provides new customer friendly Mobile ATM Vans. A van is fitted with ATM machine and it moves from place to place depending customers rush. Customers of any Bank can use their card to withdraw cash from our Mobile ATM. Presently 8 Mobile ATMs are installed by the Bank and they provide ATM services in rural areas. Mobile ATMs are also used to increase awareness about digital transactions.
  • Online Digital Account
    Our Bank is offering digital account opening through Video KYC and Instant Account opening through Aadhaar Authentication. Customer can download the app and instantly open an account.
  • ATMs & Passbook Kiosks
    Bank has deployed ATM Machines and self service Passbook printing machines (Swayam) at branch locations.
